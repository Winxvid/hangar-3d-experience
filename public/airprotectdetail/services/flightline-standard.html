Flightline Standard

Unmatched Shine and Long-Lasting Defense

The Flightline Standard Package is the ideal choice for aircraft owners seeking to restore their aircraft's exterior to like-new condition while providing long-lasting protection from environmental factors.

This foundational package delivers cleaning and short-term protection for actively flown aircraft, keeping your exterior surfaces preserved and ready for every departure.

What's Included

  • Cleaning & short-term protection
  • Exterior surface preservation
  • Quick refresh for actively flown aircraft
  • Professional-grade products and application

Ideal For

  • Aircraft Owners: If your aircraft's paint has faded, lost its gloss, or developed minor scratches, Flightline Standard is a cost-effective solution to make it look like new, extending its lifespan without the cost of a repaint.
  • Pre-Sale Preparation: Brokers or owners preparing an aircraft for sale can benefit from this service, as it enhances the aircraft's appearance and can increase resale value, making it more attractive to potential buyers.
  • High-Use or Charter Aircraft Operators: Aircraft that are frequently used or chartered endure higher wear. This package keeps these aircraft looking professional and well-maintained, ensuring they always make a positive impression on clients.
  • Weather-Exposed Aircraft: If your aircraft is exposed to the elements regularly, AirProtect signature wax protective qualities will guard against UV rays, corrosion, and environmental contaminants, reducing the impact of weather-related wear and tear.
