Flightline Elite

Comprehensive 360° Cleaning & Protection Package

The Flightline Elite Package delivers a comprehensive 360-degree cleaning and protection service for your aircraft, covering every aspect from nose to tail, inside and out.

This package includes a wet or dry wash, application of our advanced Wax protective spray coating, which lasts twice as long as traditional wax and provides superior protection and shine. The brightwork is polished to a mirror finish, enhancing the exterior's brilliance, while the interior receives a deep cleaning and detail, ensuring it looks refreshed and spotless.

Recommended every 90 days to keep your aircraft in peak condition and constantly maintained, this package guarantees long-lasting cleanliness and protection.

Services Included

  • Wet or Dry Wash
  • AirProtect Wax Protective Spray Coating
  • Polishing of Brightwork to a Mirror Finish
  • Complete Interior Deep Cleaning and Detailing

Ideal For

  • Aircraft Owners: Seeking a complete, 360-degree cleaning and protection for their aircraft's interior and exterior.
  • Special Events: Preparing aircraft for special events or important flights where appearance is critical.
  • Type-A Personalities: Those who can't live without a thorough refresh of both the exterior and interior on a regular schedule.

Benefits

  • Full 360-degree cleaning for every surface inside and out
  • Wax coating provides lasting protection and superior shine
  • Polished brightwork to a mirror finish adds brilliance to metal surfaces
  • Deep cleaning and detailing of the interior leaves it refreshed and spotless
  • Recommended every 90 days for ongoing maintenance and protection
