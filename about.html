Our Story

About AirProtect Detailing

AirProtect Detailing is a veteran-owned aircraft preservation company dedicated to protecting the value and appearance of general aviation aircraft through precision, discipline, and uncompromising standards. Founded by Tim Warner, a U.S. military veteran, AirProtect was created for aircraft owners who demand more than a basic wash — owners who understand that proper care is an investment in safety, longevity, and resale value.

We specialize in preservation-first detailing using only FAA-safe methods and aviation-grade products. Our approach is deliberately different from automotive-style detailing. We never rush the job or apply unnecessary abrasion. Instead, every aircraft receives an individual, in-person assessment followed by a tailored plan designed to minimize long-term wear while maximizing protection for paint, brightwork, windows, interiors, and critical components.

At the heart of our work is a simple belief: an aircraft is far more than a vehicle; it is a significant asset that deserves expert, respectful care. Whether your aircraft flies daily or sits in a hangar, our goal is the same — to preserve its surfaces, maintain its operational readiness, and protect its value for years to come.

All work is performed on-site at your hangar or airport by trained, insured professionals who treat every aircraft with the same attention to detail and operational rigor learned through years of military service.
