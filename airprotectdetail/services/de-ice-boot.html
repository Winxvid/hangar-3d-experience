De-Ice Boot Refurbishment

Protect and Revitalize Your Aircraft's Defenses

Maintain the efficiency and appearance of your aircraft's de-ice boots with our De-Ice Boot Refurbishment service. This specialized process removes oxidation, restores flexibility, and enhances the boots' protective coating to ensure optimal performance in icy conditions.

Keep your de-ice boots looking great and operating reliably with a service designed to extend their lifespan and functionality.

Benefits

  • Enhanced Performance: Restores the effectiveness of de-ice boots for reliable operation in icy conditions.
  • Oxidation Removal: Eliminates buildup to improve both function and appearance.
  • Extended Lifespan: Protects and preserves the material, delaying the need for costly replacements.
  • Improved Flexibility: Maintains the boots' pliability for consistent de-icing performance.
  • Refined Appearance: Provides a polished finish, enhancing your aircraft's professional look.
  • Protective Coating: Applies a durable layer to safeguard against environmental wear and damage.
  • Skilled Expertise: Service performed by trained technicians to ensure precise and effective results.
