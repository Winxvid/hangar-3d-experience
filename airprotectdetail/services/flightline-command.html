Flightline Command

Anytime, Anywhere!

Introducing Flightline Command, the ultimate subscription program designed to keep your aircraft in top condition all year long. Choose between quarterly or twice-a-year services and enjoy complete interior and exterior detailing, plus touch-up services to keep your aircraft looking its best between major cleanings.

A flat monthly fee provides owners and operators peace of mind, ensuring their aircraft cleanings are part of a proactive, tracked program. This approach not only keeps the aircraft consistently maintained but also allows for a predictable and manageable cleaning budget.

What You Get with Flightline Command

Complete Exterior Cleaning & Brightwork Polishing

Each service includes an exterior wash, brightwork polishing to a mirror finish, and the application of our wax protective coating to preserve shine and protect your aircraft's paint.

Complete Interior Deep Cleaning & Detail

Whether you choose quarterly or twice-a-year service, enjoy a thorough interior cleaning, including stain removal, upholstery care, and sanitization of all surfaces.

AirProtect Wax Protective Coating Application

This premium coating enhances gloss, provides long-lasting protection, and makes future cleanings easier by preventing dirt and debris from sticking to the surface.

Touch-Up Services Between Cleanings

Keep your aircraft looking flawless with touch-up services in between your scheduled major cleanings.

Key Benefits

  • Multi-Year Preservation Program: Long-term value retention with consistent, scheduled maintenance
  • Comprehensive Surface Protection: Full interior and exterior coverage from nose to tail
  • Priority Scheduling: As a Command subscriber, your aircraft receives priority booking
  • Predictable Budget: Flat monthly fee eliminates surprise costs and simplifies planning
  • Proactive Maintenance: Tracked program ensures nothing falls through the cracks
  • Flexible Service Frequency: Choose quarterly or twice-a-year based on your needs
