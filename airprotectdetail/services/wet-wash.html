Wet Wash

Flawless Results Even in the Harshest Conditions

At AirProtect, our Wet Wash service combines precision, premium products, and expert care to restore your aircraft to pristine condition. Designed for aircraft exposed to harsh environments, including saltwater regions, our meticulous process removes dirt, debris, and contaminants while protecting your investment.

With a commitment to excellence, our wet washing service ensures your aircraft shines with a flawless, streak-free finish, ready to impress at every takeoff and landing.

Benefits

  • Thorough Exterior Cleaning: Removes dirt, grime, and contaminants with precision.
  • Corrosion Protection: Effectively eliminates salt and residues that can damage your aircraft.
  • Streak-Free Finish: Delivers a spotless, polished look every time.
  • Safe for All Surfaces: Gentle yet effective, suitable for any aircraft exterior.
  • Enhanced Longevity: Protects paint and coatings, extending your aircraft's finish life.
  • Ideal for Demanding Environments: Perfect for high-exposure regions such as coastal and industrial areas.
